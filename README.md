## üïπÔ∏è How to Play

1. **Select a Piece:** Click on any white piece to select it. The square will highlight in purple.
2. **Move:** Click on a legal destination square to complete the move.
3. **AI Turn:** After your move, the AI will automatically calculate and respond within a few milliseconds.
4. **Reset:** Use the "Reset" button to clear the board and move history at any time.
